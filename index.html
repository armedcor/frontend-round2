CORMAC LAWLOR

OPERATIC BARITONE

About

Previously a member of the Northern Ireland Opera studio, Cormac also completed a term as a Young Associated Artist with Opera Theatre Company in Dublin before going on to work with companies such as Irish National Opera, Northern Ireland Opera, Wexford Festival Opera, Glyndebourne Opera Festival and Opera Holland Park.

Cormac was named the 2017 PWC Young Emerging Artist with Wexford Festival Opera as well as the inaugural recipient of the Liam Healy Bursary.



Operatic roles include Farrington in Synnott’s Dubliners with Opera Theatre Company, Bonze in Puccini’s Madama Butterfly with RTE NCO, Colline in Puccini’s La Boheme with Lyric Opera and Cork Operatic Society, 5th Jew in Strauss Salome with NI Opera, the King of Egypt in Verdi’s Aida, Kromow in Lehar’s The Merry Widow, An Old Gypsy in Verdi’s Il Trovatore and Baron Douphol in Verdi’s La Traviata with Lyric Opera Productions.
